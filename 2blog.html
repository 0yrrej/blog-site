Amazon

Computational Artifact

2a)
Amazon was intentionally created as an online book store. However, Amazon shifted away from only books to what is now the largest e-commerce marketplace. Amazon is able to connect worldwide merchandisers with consumers by playing the middleman in product storage, marketing, and delivery. Firstly, Amazon stores products for sellers in warehouses in adjacent to posting the products on their website. Secondly, Amazon is able to market various types of products through algorithms that advertise to all types of consumers. Lastly, Amazon appeals to consumers through its fast delivery options. My artifact shows visuals representing Amazons function on the e-commerce marketplace.

2c)
Amazons impacted on way consumers and merchandisers interact has changed with the introduction of Amazon's delivery service. To begin with, Amazon delivers six days a week, which can deliver items within a few hours along with extra fees. People would rather pay these extra fees to have products delivered to the them. This fast delivery service is preferred by people who do not want to leave their home to purchase a product. The convenience of not having to leave your home or interact with an employee is what interests people to use the service. In addition, as source 1 says, Amazon helps connect small businesses with world wide consumers without the responsibility of delivery. Amazon's delivery service assists in quick delivery from most sellers to consumers. This gives the customers a wider scope of product options that is accumulated and verified on a single website. Consequently Amazon has improved the way consumers find products from world wide merchandisers.

On the other hand, Amazon has negatively impacted retail stores. Since Amazon's intended purpose is to provide creative online alternative to product purchasing, it has left retail stores behind. As source 3 say, traditional retail stores have seen a decline is purchases. This is due to Amazon's creative efficient services and competitive prices. Consequently, traditional retail stores have had to close many of their stores in efforts to lower expenses.

2d)
Amazon uses customer data from interests, searches, and demographics to recommend various products. To expand, the data that Amazon consumes is collected in a profile that improves product advertising. These profiles are matched with other similar profiles to improve the algorithm that promotes products.
